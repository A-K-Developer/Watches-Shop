Welcome to our monthly watch program, where we are partnering with Red Cross to support important causes around the world. With every watch you purchase, you'll not only add a stylish timepiece to your collection, but you'll also be contributing to a worthy cause.

We believe in making a difference, and that's why we've committed to supporting different causes every month. Based on the number of watches sold, we will donate a portion of our proceeds to a specific cause. When you purchase between 50-100 watches, we will support one cause, and when you purchase between 100-200 watches, we'll support a second cause. And for those who purchase over 200 watches, we'll have a special limited edition watch available, and all proceeds from those sales will go towards the third cause.
